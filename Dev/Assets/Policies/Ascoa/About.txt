<h1>Who We are?</h1>
<p>Ash Shukur Charity and Orphanage Aid is a Muslim Founded Charity organisation that is Aimed at helping all Suffering Orphans and Eldery People .</p>

<p>The organisation was Founded by a group of committed muslim Brothers and Sisters but currently managed by Isiko Ibrahim who is a more self reliant and ambitious Person who has the drive to help change community lives and has big visions for charity organisation.</p
